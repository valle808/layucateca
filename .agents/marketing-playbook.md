# Marketing Playbook — La Yucateca News Channel

> Last updated by Campaign Director agent. Read product-marketing.md first for channel context.

## Executive Summary

La Yucateca is an AI-powered, bilingual (EN/ES) news channel built on Next.js with Muna AI as its embedded AI assistant. The channel targets modern news consumers in Latin America, the US Hispanic market, and Spanish-speaking global audience aged 25-45.

**Current Stage**: Growth Phase — building audience and establishing distribution channels.

---

## 90-Day Marketing Roadmap

### Month 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
**Owner**: Campaign Director + SEO Specialist

- [ ] Submit to Google News publisher center

- [ ] Monthly report: full metrics review + pivots

## SEO Strategy

### Primary Keyword Clusters

#### Technology & AI
- "AI news [year]"
- "artificial intelligence latest news"
- "tech news today"
- "startup news"

#### Latin America / Regional
- "Latin America news"
- "Mexico news today"
- "Yucatan news"
- "noticias de hoy"

#### General News
- "breaking news today"
- "world news [date]"
- "news summary"

### Programmatic SEO Pages to Build
- `/news/topic/[category]` — 20 main topic pages
- `/news/location/[country]` — Country-specific news hubs
- `/news/person/[name]` — Notable person pages
- `/news/organization/[name]` — Organization coverage hubs
- `/news/archive/[year]/[month]` — Date archives

### Schema Markup Requirements
All articles must have:
- `NewsArticle` schema
- `BreadcrumbList` schema
- `Person` schema for authors
- `Organization` schema for publisher

## Social Media Content Pillars

### La Yucateca Content Pillars
1. **Breaking News** (35%) — First with facts, context within minutes
2. **AI Perspective** (20%) — "Here's what Muna AI thinks about this"
3. **Explainers** (20%) — "What is [topic]? A 60-second breakdown"
4. **Behind the Story** (15%) — How we cover the news
5. **Community** (10%) — Reader questions, polls, local stories

## Paid Advertising Playbook

### Phase 1: Test ($500/month)
Objective: Find the most efficient subscriber acquisition channel.

Budget allocation:
- 70% Facebook/Instagram (subscriber acquisition)
- 20% Google Search ("news" + local keywords)
- 10% Retargeting (site visitors who didn't subscribe)

KPI target: Cost per newsletter subscriber < $2.00

### Winning Ad Creative Formulas

**Facebook/Instagram**:
Headline: "Stay informed with La Yucateca"
Body: "Join 3,000+ readers who get AI-powered news analysis every morning. Free newsletter. Unsubscribe anytime."
CTA: "Subscribe Free"
Creative: Clean design with "Daily News Briefing" text and newsletter preview

**Google Search** (when budget allows):
Keywords: "[city] news today", "latin america news", "AI news digest"
Headline: "Today's Top News — Free Daily Briefing"
Description: "AI-powered news coverage. 5-minute daily digest. Join 3,000+ readers."
