## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- **Age**: 25-45
- **Location**: Latin America + US Hispanic market + Global Spanish speakers
- **Interests**: Current events, technology, business, politics, culture
- **Behavior**: Mobile-first news consumers, social media active, newsletter readers
- **Pain point**: Too much news, not enough signal. Overwhelmed by misinformation.

### Secondary Audience
- English-speaking tech professionals and news enthusiasts
- Journalists, analysts, researchers using Muna AI for research
- Digital nomads and internationally-minded professionals

## Brand Voice & Tone

**Tone**: Authoritative yet approachable. Smart without being elitist. Fast without being careless.

**Writing style**:
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max in news)
- Active voice always
- Facts first, context second, opinion clearly labeled
- Bilingual content where appropriate (don't just translate — localize)

**Avoid**:
- Clickbait headlines
- Sensationalism without substance
- Political bias in news reporting (clear opinion labeling)
- Jargon without explanation
